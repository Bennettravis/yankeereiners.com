Joining the YRHA

YRHA Criteria -

To qualify for Year End Awards, the rider & owner must both be members of YRHA prior to showing. The rider/horse combination must also show at 50% of the YRHA run show held during the year in order to qualify for Year End Awards. The rider must be a member in order to show in YRHA’s Green Reiner program.

YRHA Affiliate Designation

These are the current Affiliate Member Roster for YRHA.

If your name says "TRUE" you have designated Yankee as your Affiliate.

Those that say "NONE", have never completed a designation and need to do so if they want to ride for YRHA this October in Syracuse!

If there is a different Affiliate listed by your name you can only show for that Affiliate.
